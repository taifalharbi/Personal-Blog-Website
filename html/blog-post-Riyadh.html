A Guide to the Best Attractions and Experiences

Riyadh is the capital city of Saudi Arabia, located in the central region of the country. It is a modern and bustling city, known for its high-rise buildings, luxury shopping malls, and rich cultural heritage..

Here are some of the top places to visit in Riyadh:

  • Al Masmak Fortress: This historic fortress was built in the 19th century and is now a museum that showcases the history of the city and the country.
  • Kingdom Centre Tower: This iconic skyscraper is one of the tallest buildings in Riyadh and offers stunning views of the city from its observation deck.
  • Al Rajhi Grand Mosque:This grand mosque is one of the largest in the world and is known for its beautiful architecture and intricate design.
  • Riyadh Gallery Mall: This large shopping mall features a wide variety of stores, restaurants, and entertainment options.
